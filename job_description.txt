# Data Analyst - TechFlow Solutions

## Company Overview
TechFlow Solutions is a fast-growing fintech company that provides innovative payment processing solutions to small and medium-sized businesses. We're looking for a talented Data Analyst to join our analytics team and help drive data-driven decision making across the organization.

## Position Summary
We are seeking a detail-oriented Data Analyst to transform raw data into actionable business insights. You will work closely with cross-functional teams to analyze customer behavior, optimize business processes, and support strategic initiatives through comprehensive data analysis and reporting.

## Key Responsibilities

### Data Analysis & Reporting
- Extract, clean, and analyze large datasets from multiple sources including databases, APIs, and third-party platforms
- Create comprehensive reports and dashboards to track key performance indicators (KPIs) and business metrics
- Perform statistical analysis to identify trends, patterns, and anomalies in business data
- Develop automated reporting solutions to streamline regular business reviews

### Business Intelligence
- Partner with stakeholders across Marketing, Sales, Product, and Operations to understand analytical needs
- Translate business questions into analytical frameworks and methodologies
- Present findings and recommendations to senior leadership through clear visualizations and presentations
- Monitor and analyze customer acquisition, retention, and lifetime value metrics

### Data Management
- Ensure data quality and integrity across reporting systems
- Document data sources, methodologies, and analytical processes
- Collaborate with Engineering team to improve data collection and storage processes
- Maintain and optimize existing SQL queries and data pipelines

## Required Qualifications

### Education & Experience
- Bachelor's degree in Data Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Economics, or related analytical field
- 2-4 years of experience in data analysis, business intelligence, or related role
- Experience working with large datasets and complex data structures

### Technical Skills
- **Advanced SQL proficiency** - complex queries, joins, window functions, and performance optimization
- **Python or R** - data manipulation, statistical analysis, and automation
- **Data visualization tools** - Tableau, Power BI, or similar platforms
- **Statistical analysis** - hypothesis testing, regression analysis, A/B testing
- **Excel/Google Sheets** - advanced formulas, pivot tables, and data modeling

### Analytical Skills
- Strong problem-solving abilities with attention to detail
- Experience with experimental design and statistical inference
- Ability to work with ambiguous requirements and define analytical approaches
- Understanding of business metrics and KPI development

## Preferred Qualifications

### Advanced Technical Skills
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure) and their analytics services
- Knowledge of machine learning concepts and applications
- Familiarity with API integration and data extraction
- Experience with version control systems (Git)

### Domain Knowledge
- Previous experience in fintech, payments, or e-commerce industries
- Understanding of customer analytics and behavioral segmentation
- Knowledge of financial metrics and regulatory reporting requirements

### Soft Skills
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills
- Experience presenting to executive-level stakeholders
- Ability to work independently and manage multiple projects simultaneously
- Strong collaboration skills in cross-functional team environments
