Job Description: Field and Forest Resource Officer

Overview
We are seeking a Field and Forest Resource Officer to support sustainable forest management and conservation initiatives. The ideal candidate will be proactive, analytical, and committed to preserving natural ecosystems through effective resource monitoring, data collection, and community engagement.

Key Responsibilities
Conduct field surveys, forest inventories, and resource assessments to gather accurate environmental data.
Monitor forest reserves to prevent illegal logging, wildfires, and other environmental threats.
Support pre-felling and post-felling operations, ensuring adherence to forestry regulations.
Collect, analyze, and interpret forestry data to support planning and decision-making.
Inspect forest products and transport operations for compliance with legal and sustainability standards.
Participate in community outreach programs promoting sustainable forest use and conservation.
Prepare technical reports and documentation based on field findings.
Collaborate with forestry teams, government agencies, and local communities to implement resource management strategies.
